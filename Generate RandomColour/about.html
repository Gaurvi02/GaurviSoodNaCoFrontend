About Color Models

Understanding Color Models

Color models are abstract mathematical models describing the way colors can be represented as tuples of numbers, typically as three or four values or color components.

RGB Color Model

The RGB color model is an additive color model in which red, green, and blue light are added together in various ways to reproduce a broad array of colors.

CMY Color Model

The CMY color model is a subtractive color model used in color printing. This model creates colors by mixing cyan, magenta, and yellow inks.

Web Safe Colors

Web safe colors were developed during the early days of the internet to ensure consistent color display across different computers and browsers.
